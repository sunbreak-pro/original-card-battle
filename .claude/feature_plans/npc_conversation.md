## 1. Overview

NPC conversation system planned for the Guild facility. This feature enhances world-building and provides gameplay information through character interactions.

## 2. Planned Features

### 2.1 Information Gathering

- Conversation with NPCs in the Guild tavern
- Rumors and hints about dungeon exploration
- Lore and world-building through dialogue

### 2.2 Social Interaction

- Regular NPCs with recurring dialogue
- Story-related conversations
- Gameplay tips disguised as NPC dialogue

## 3. Integration Points

- **Facility**: Guild (Headquarters tab)
- **Context**: May require new NPCContext or extend GuildContext
- **UI**: Dialogue overlay system

## 4. Implementation Priority

**Priority**: Low
**Phase**: Post-MVP (Phase 3+)
**Dependencies**: Core Guild functionality must be stable
